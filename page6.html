What I remember most:

October 29, 2025 – Azure FrontDoor global outage. The service was completely down and our web app became inaccessible. I applied a temporary workaround by adding Azure Traffic Manager in front of FrontDoor (normally, Microsoft recommends using either FrontDoor or Traffic Manager, not both). Our service availability was restored within 30 minutes, minimizing user impact. I remember this most because it was an "all the dots connected" moment - my prior study of the architecture pattern paid off under real-world pressure.
